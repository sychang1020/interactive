                                                                   Did you know they use
                                                         microscopic images to depict space?
                                                 What im saying is that you can find me inside everything small,
                                     and everything small equals                                    everything big.
                                             What I'm saying is that                             the space                   between you and I
                                       should be microscopic—                                            smaller, even.
                                               But there it is always,                                that gaping whistle of air molecules
                                                         between our hands,                   between our fingers and our cigarettes,
                                                                 an entire galaxy enclosed in our palms,
                                                                         no matter how hard we clap
